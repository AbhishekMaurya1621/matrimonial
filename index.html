About Us

In the Indian ethos of matchmaking, marriage is a sacred bond not just between two individuals, but also an alliance of two families that come together to share rituals, customs, festivities, duties and responsibilities. In a country with such a vast array of cultures and religions including Hindus, Punjabis, Aggarwals, Jains, Baniyas, Sikhs, Brahmins, Jains, Marwaris, Rajputs, Muslims, Christians and various other castes, a profound knowledge of their customs and traditions is paramount for carrying out the requisite partner search.

We, at Wedgate Matrimony, with our team of in house dedicated Marriage Counsellors and Relationship Managers, with years of expertise and patience in dealing with a cross section of society, are your gateway to that perfect partner that you envisage for yourself or your dear child, friend, relative, niece, nephew, grandchild, etc. Be it in any corner of the world, we have just that magical profile that will transform your life and relationship status, forever!

So, if it’s marriage on your mind, the only gate that you need to enter is WEDGATE to make your dream companion, a flesh and blood reality in person. Welcome to Wedgate Matrimony.
